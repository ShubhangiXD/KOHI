Recipe:

1. Add 1.5 teaspoons instant coffee powder, 2 teaspoons water and 2 teaspoons sugar in a coffee cup or mug.2. Mix well. Beat the mixture until light and creamy.3. Bring milk to a boil and add to the creamy coffee sugar mixture.4. Top with cocoa powder and serve espresso coffee immediately.

Doppio

Recipe:

1. Grind your beans into a consistency that passes through your machine.2. Make two separate amounts that will fit into your portafilter and tamp the contents so they are lightly packed. 3. If you have a hand pump espresso maker, youll need to use several repeated pumps to build up pressure. This not only allows the pressure to build while the espresso is filtering, but it adds a richer crema that gives espresso more character. 4. When youre done you simply add sugar or milk to your doppio and then drink right away.

Macchiato

Recipe:

1. Brew one shot of fresh espresso with espresso machine.2. Steam milk to 160, and hold the steam wand just below the surface to create foam.3. Pour milk into espresso to taste and spoon foam on top.
